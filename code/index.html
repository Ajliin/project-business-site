FOAJÉ is the place for women to start up or accelerate their own business in their own way and pace. “How can we make women more willing to take risk?” FOAJÉ thinks it is the wrong way to approach this question.

FOAJÉ also acts as a community and commercial space where woman can sell and buy products and services to and from each other. It fits for you no matter if it is your hobby, part time or if you want to do it full time. Create your profile and let the community know about your competences and what your conditions are.

At FOAJÉ you are of course also welcome only to search for services and products sold on the platform from another female entrepreneur.
